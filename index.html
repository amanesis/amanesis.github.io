About Me

Hello, I'm Manesis Athanasios, an ECE engineer currently pursuing my thesis at the Technical University of Crete. I have a passion for technology, particularly in the field of embedded systems and robotics.

One of my main hobbies is mountain activities and hiking, which allows me to explore nature and challenge myself physically. I find inspiration and a sense of tranquility in the great outdoors.

My current focus is on developing an embedded gimbal system for land-based tracking of UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles). This project combines my interests in embedded systems, robotics, and tracking technology.

Throughout my academic journey, I have gained a strong foundation in electrical engineering, programming, and problem-solving. I am constantly seeking new opportunities to expand my knowledge and apply it to real-world projects.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and projects to learn more about my work and interests. Thank you for visiting my GitHub Pages site!
